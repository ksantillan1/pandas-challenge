Trend1: Performing Schools by budget and Size

The budget assigned to the bottoms schools are a lot larger than the budget assigned to the top, 
and that is not only because of the bottom performers school size being larger. 
When breaking down by budget per student you can observe that student with lower spending budget are the higher performers. 
There is also a relationship between size and performance. The larger the school is, the lower the performance is

Trend2: Performing Schools by Type

There were two type of schools in the data, District and charter, 
and we be observed that the performance of the Charter type of school is higher than the performance of the District schools.
With an overall passing rate of 95% comparing to 73%.
